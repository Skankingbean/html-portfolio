The Best Video Games According to Louie

Top Three Video Games of All Time

Kingdom Heart 1 & 2

Captivating Story revolving around wonder, friendship, and loss. Recognizable and interesting characters from both Disney and Square Enix I.P.'s make the experience of both game truly breathtaking. Levels are set in the universes of various Disney I.P.'s.

Castlevania: Symphony of the Night

Action packed gameplay that begins as soon as the game is launched. This game radiates a sense of lost and exploration not seen in the Castlevania series before up until this point. Well hidden secrets and RPG elements give the game massive ammounts of replayability.

Dark Souls 3

Massive learning curve for anyone who has never played a FromSoft game. The difficulty throughout the game gives a feeling of accomplishment and reward for completeing levels or defeating bosses. The game can be replayed multiple times with different builds to give the player a truly unique experience with each playthrough.
